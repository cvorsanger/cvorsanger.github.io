About

Corey is passionate about finding the hiden insights cotained in large datasets. He firmly believes in using these insights to bring about change and better and smarter business practices. Currently, he is wrking towards earning a Masters of Science degree in Data Science at the University of Denver. Corey also has both a Bachelors and a Masters degree from Arizona State University in Aerospace Engineering.

Corey has completed data science projets in both Python and R, though he is more primarily uses Python. Additionally, he has completed various other work in Matlab, Fortran, and Excel VBA.

Outside of Data Science Corey loves to explore Northern Utah. In the summer you can frequently find him hiking in the Uinta of Wasatch Mountains. In the winter he is typically on his snowboard finding powder stashes.

Projects

Sentiement Analysis of Olympic tweets

Analyzed tweets regarding the sports of track, gymnastics, cycling, volleyball, diving, basketball, surfing, and skateboarding during the 2020 Olympic Games. Using Python, the NLTK library, and the VADER lexicon the sentiment of tweets were scored.

Movie Genre Predictions with Naive Bayes

Using R, predicted movies from their viewer created plots. Transformed the plots into a bag-of-words and created a multiclass Naive Bayes model. Was able to achieve an accuracy of 85% with this simple model.

The Affect of COVID-19 on Airports

Analyzed the effect of COVID-19 on the United States airport system. Created a CLI tool to help analyze airports individualy, by state, or by region. Was also able to make conclusions based on the type of airport.
